## 1. Introduction

The TMC Portal is your central hub for engaging with the community, staying informed about news and events, and managing your membership.

---

## 2. Joining the Community

### Creating an Account
1. Visit the portal homepage and click on **Sign Up**.
2. Provide your name, email address, and choose a secure password.
3. Verify your email address through the link sent to your inbox.

### Membership Application
After creating an account, you need to apply for membership:
1. Navigate to the **Member Dashboard**.
2. Click on **Become a Member** or **Apply for Membership**.
3. Fill out the application form with your personal details and select your preferred branch/organization.
4. Submit the form.

### Application Status
You can track your application status on your dashboard:
- **PENDING**: Your application is being reviewed by the branch.
- **ACTIVE**: Your application has been approved, and you are now a member!

---

## 3. Your Member Dashboard

Once your membership is active, your dashboard becomes your command center:
- **Member ID**: Your unique identifier within the organization.
- **Membership Level**: View whether you are a Regular, Associate, or Honorary member.
- **Branch Information**: Details about your local chapter and its leadership.
- **Payment History**: A list of all your fee payments and donations.

## 4. Engaging in Meetings

### Finding Meetings
Stay involved by attending local and state meetings:
1. Go to the **Meetings** section in your dashboard.
2. View the calendar of upcoming scheduled meetings.

### Digital Check-in
For online or hybrid meetings:
1. Click on the meeting title to view details.
2. Use the **Join Meeting** button to mark your attendance and access the meeting link.

### Member Reports
If you are required to submit a report for a meeting:
1. Navigate to the specific meeting page.
2. Use the **Submit Report** feature to upload your document or enter text.
3. *Note: Reports submitted after the deadline (2 days before the meeting) will be marked as "LATE".*

### Minutes & Agendas
Access official records of past meetings and prepare for upcoming ones by downloading Agendas and Minutes from the meeting details page.

## 5. Events & Programmes

### Browsing Events
Explore spiritual, educational, and social events:
1. Go to the **Programmes** section.
2. View detailed descriptions, dates, and venues for upcoming events like Seminars or Camping trips.

### Registration
1. Click **Register** on an event page to secure your spot.
2. If the event requires a fee, you will be prompted to make a secure payment via Paystack.

## 6. Special Requests (Occasions)

Members can request the organization to officiate or support family events:
- **Nikkah (Marriage)**: Request officiating and marriage coaching.
- **Naming Ceremony**: Request support for naming ceremonies.
- **Burial Support**: Urgent requests for funeral assistance and prayers.

### How to Request:
1. Navigate to the **Occasions** or **Burial** section.
2. Fill out the request form with the date, venue, and required details.
3. Track the approval status and assigned officiant on your dashboard.
4. Upon completion, you may be issued a digital certificate.

## 7. Public Resources & Contributions

### Stay Updated
- **News**: Read the latest updates from across the organization on the Home page.
- **Gallery**: View photos from recent events and gatherings.

### Resources
- **Constitution**: Access the organization's governing documents online.
- **Executives Page**: Learn about the officials serving at various levels.

### Contributions
Support the organization's missions:
- **Donations**: Use the **Donate** page to make one-time or recurring contributions.
- **Membership Dues**: Pay your annual dues conveniently through the portal. Payments are automatically routed to your branch's bank account if they have configured a subaccount.

### Media Library (`/dashboard/member/programmes`)
Access high-quality spiritual and educational resources.
- Listen to or watch Friday Khuthbahs, Teskiyah Workshops, and Press Releases.
- Download documents and study materials for offline use.

## 8. Support

### Updating Your Profile
Keep your contact information up to date by visiting the **Profile Settings** page.

### Getting Help
If you encounter any issues or have questions:
1. Check the **FAQs** section (if available).
